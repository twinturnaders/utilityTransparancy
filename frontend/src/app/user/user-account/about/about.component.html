About This Project

This platform was built to make water and sewer rates more transparent across Montana communities. Many small towns face high bills, confusing late fees, and aggressive shut-off policies. By sharing and comparing information, we can give residents the tools they need to understand their utility costs and advocate for fairer rates.

Crowdsourcing Rates

A key part of this project is you. If you have a bill from your town, you can enter your rates here. The more people who contribute, the stronger and more accurate the database becomes. Even if you donâ€™t live in a town but can look up its posted rates online, entering those helps others too.

Confidence Ratings

Each submission includes a star-based confidence rating. This helps everyone see whether a rate came directly from a bill (higher confidence) or from another source (lower confidence). Over time, this system makes the data more reliable and trustworthy.

Compare Your Usage

Wondering how your bill stacks up? You can compare your own monthly usage with other towns. For example, if you used 5000 gal of water this month, you can instantly see what that same amount would have cost in neighboring communities. This makes it easy to spot which places have higher or lower rates, and how much those differences really add up to.

Together, small contributions make a big difference. Entering just one set of rates could help a neighbor, a nearby town, or an entire region better understand their costs.
