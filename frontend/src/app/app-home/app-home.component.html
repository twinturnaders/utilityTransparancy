Welcome to Utility Transparency

Bringing clarity to Montana's municipal water and sewer rates.

Why This Matters

Across Montana, water and sewer rates vary dramatically from town to town—with little transparency or public access to rate structures. Many residents pay high bills without knowing how those costs compare to neighboring communities, or how they’re calculated.

How We Get the Data

This platform is crowdsourced. That means we rely on residents like you to submit bills, rate sheets, or ordinance data from your town. It’s the only way we can gather accurate, up-to-date information across all municipalities.

Together, we can build a better picture of fairness, cost, and access.

Understanding the Confidence Rating

Each municipality is given a confidence rating from 1 to 5 stars:

  • ★– Based on state-level (DNRC) or outdated data
  • ★★ – Inferred or partial public info
  • ★★★ – One user-submitted bill
  • ★★★★ – Two or more consistent submissions
  • ★★★★★ – Verified by admin with ordinance or full review

Track Your Bills Over Time

Create a free account to securely save your water and sewer bills. Your personal dashboard lets you track changes in your costs, compare against neighboring towns, and build a history of your household utility expenses.

By saving bills, you also contribute to our crowdsourced database, helping neighbors across Montana gain better insight into their rates.
